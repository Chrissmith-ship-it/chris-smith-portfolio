Example: Month end pack helper
Small snippet from my workflow, shown at a readable size. It rolls actuals versus budget into a simple variance view.
Show the snippet 
import pandas as pd

df = pd.read_csv("actuals.csv")
bud = pd.read_csv("budget.csv")

m = df.merge(bud, on=["cost_center","account"], suffixes=("_act","_bud"))
m["variance"] = m["amount_act"] - m["amount_bud"]

print(
  m.groupby("cost_center")["variance"]
   .sum()
   .sort_values(ascending=False)
   .head(10)
)
Why this matters
Before automation, building the monthly pack took ~6 hours of manual Excel work with version control risk. Now it runs in under 2 minutes. The validation catches errors that used to slip through. And because it's standardised, the output is always consistent - which matters when you're presenting to ExCo.
